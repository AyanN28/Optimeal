OptiMeal AI
Smart food choices, guided by your health.
📖 The Problem
Healthy eating is often a "Location & Time" problem, not just a willpower problem. Most apps tell you what is healthy, but they don't know where you are or how much time you have. This leads to "decision fatigue," causing users to settle for fast, unhealthy options.

🚀 The Solution
OptiMeal AI is a hyper-local, context-aware nutrition assistant. It bridges the gap between high-level AI reasoning and real-world availability. By syncing your Health DNA with Google Maps and Google Calendar, it provides actionable meal interventions exactly when and where you need them.

✨ Key Features
🥗 Hyper-Local Compass: Uses Google Places API to find nearby restaurants matching your specific Health DNA (Keto, Diabetic-friendly, etc.).

👁️ Visual Nutrition Audit: Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash Vision. Snap a photo of a menu or grocery label to get an instant health score and smarter alternatives.

📅 Smart Schedule Sync: Integrates with Google Calendar. Busy back-to-back meetings? OptiMeal suggests a 5-minute protein bowl. Free evening? It suggests a full, healthy recipe.

🌦️ Contextual Logic: Real-time adjustments based on time and weather (e.g., increased hydration alerts during heatwaves).

🔐 Privacy-First (Zero-Footprint): All Health DNA is stored in the browser's LocalStorage. No databases, no logins, no tracking.
